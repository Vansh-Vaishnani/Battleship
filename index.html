How to Play Battleship

Objective

Sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours!

Fleet

  • Carrier: 5 cells
  • Battleship: 4 cells
  • Cruiser: 3 cells
  • Submarine: 3 cells
  • Destroyer: 2 cells

Gameplay

  1. Choose Game Mode: Select vs Computer or Two Player
  2. Place Ships: Drag and drop ships onto your board. Click a ship to rotate it before placing.
  3. Take Turns: Click on the enemy board to attack. A red cell means you hit a ship, white means you missed.
  4. Win: First player to sink all enemy ships wins!

Strategy Tips

  • Ships cannot overlap or go outside the board
  • Once all ships are placed, click "Start Game"
  • In Two Player mode, pass the device between turns
  • Try to find patterns when you get a hit!
